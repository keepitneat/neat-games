How to play
  1. Tap a square to reveal it. Numbers show how many mines touch that square.
  2. Flag squares you think are mines: right-click, long-press, or switch to flag mode.
  3. Tap a revealed number with the right flags around it to clear the rest (chording).
  4. Clear every safe square to win. Hit a mine and it's over.
